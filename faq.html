FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When is Bernaroo?

When is Bernaroo?

On July 20th and July 21st, the New Bern Farmer's Market will house six bands a night, ranging from Southern rock and hip-hop to reggae and Americana with a little bit of everything in between. Evening shows will be $15 for a night or $25 for two nights. Doors open and music kicks off at 5:00 sharp each evening.

Where is Bernaroo?

Where is Bernaroo?

Our marquee events will take place at the New Bern Farmer's Market (421 S Front St, New Bern, NC). After our Farmer's Market Festivities, the party continues each night for free programming at The Brown Pelican (411 Broad St., New Bern, NC). During the day on July 21st, the streets of downtown New Bern will be filled with free live music, visual art, and more outside in the Beer Garden at Bear Town Market (402 S Front St, New Bern, NC).

Is Bernaroo Family Friendly?

Is Bernaroo Family Friendly?

Of course! We aim to have entertainment for children and adults of all ages. Admission is free for children 12 & under, and we will have programming and activites geared specifically for children in attendance.

Can I Bring Outside Alcohol/Food?

Can I Bring Outside Alcohol/Food?

No outside food or alcohol will be permitted for the event. We'll have fantastic local beer and wine on hand for attendees that are of age, and a slew of great food vendors as well to accommodate most dietary needs.

How Can I Get Involved?

How Can I Get Involved?

See that neat little 'Contact' button up there on the far right in the navigation menu? Click that! Whether you're an artist, volunteer or potential vendor, we'd be glad to hear from you and see how you can become a part of the Bernaroo family!

Can I buy tickets in person?

Can I buy tickets in person?

No, at this time tickets may only be purchased online. To see purchasing options, click Tickets in the navigation menu above.
