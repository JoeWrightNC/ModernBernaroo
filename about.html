ABOUT BERNAROO

Bernaroo Music & Arts Festival is an annual 2-day event organized by GEM Productions and hosted by downtown New Bern. Each July this local celebration features the best of the North Carolina music scene while shining a spotlight on the various businesses, vendors, and artisans that help make New Bern a uniquely special place. Bernaroo thrives on its ability to provide diverse and affordable entertainment to people from across the demographic spectrum.
